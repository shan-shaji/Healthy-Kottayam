Take Control of your Health.

Healthy Kottayam Website and Mobile App initiated by the District Administration Kottayam is to help people to fight against the COVID-19 (Corona Virus). Health Department officials of Kottayam district will make use of the Healthy Kottayam Mobile App to report the daily status of affected as well as quarantined people in all panchayats and Municipalities of the district. Centralized monitoring of data and coordination support will be provided with the help of call centre at the district head for its users.
